Autism Spectrum Disorder Tests App

  • About the Autism Tests App

    This app is solely for research purposes and not for commercial use. The app contains Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) tests to enable parents, care givers, and academic researchers access them. These tests are not diagnostic tools rather they are behavioural tests that just pinpoint to autistic traits. The app can be freely downloaded and distributed but cannot be used for financial gain. This app was developed by Dr Fadi Fayez Thabtah at NMIT.

Terms Of Use

Test license :

Since this app is for non-commercial and only used for research purposes. This fulfils the ARC tests terms of use.

Translation terms:

Translations of tests in this app are based on the understanding that the translation is based carefully on the original English language version published by its prospective authors. The quality of the translation lies with the translator. However, native speakers as a way of checking the quality did back-translation. Researchers who use a translated test and discover errors are encouraged to contact us at info@asdtests.com
